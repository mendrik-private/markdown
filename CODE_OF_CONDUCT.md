# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project should be a respectful, focused place to discuss and improve the software. Be direct and technical, but keep feedback constructive.

Expected behavior:

- Be respectful of different experience levels and viewpoints.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused on the work.
- Give actionable feedback and assume good intent.
- Credit ideas, reports, and contributions appropriately.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Repeated off-topic disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Abusive or hostile review behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation for behavior that harms the project or its contributors.

If you need to report a concern, contact the project maintainer through the repository owner account. Reports will be handled with care and only shared as needed to resolve the issue.
